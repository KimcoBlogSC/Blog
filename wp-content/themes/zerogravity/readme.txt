== Description ==
ZeroGravity is a simple and light WordPress theme with a clear and neat design. Some its features are: left sidebar or right, custom theme color (blue, green, orange or pink), custom favicon, thumbnails rounded or squared, two widgets areas (beginning and end of posts), customization panel, six different Google Fonts, fully responsive, custom header, custom background and more. Translation Ready (English and spanish integrated) Required WordPress 4.1+.
